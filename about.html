What's this thing?

HSL has a service where you can subscribe to their public transportation routes and get emails when there are disruptions on those lines.

They also have a Twitter Bot that tweets information about all the disruptions. But they don't have a service that tweets directly to you when there is a disruption on a line that is important to you.

This is where <insert name> comes in. Just type the name of the transportation line in the search field, click it and press the Twitter button to send a tweet to our bot and BAM, you're subscribed!
